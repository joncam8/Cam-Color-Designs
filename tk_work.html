The Story

TKerr is a personal project of mine that I decide to design and develop for showcasing work. The website showcase a inspiring Graphic Designers work and to purchase his work. The designer is a good friend of mine and allowed me to use his art work to display in the site so that I can showcase my web design abilities. Although this is only a personal project at the moment, this site is in production for the future to be an ecommerce site to purchase art work. This site was created from scratch in Photoshop and developed in Dreamweaver using HTML, CSS, BOOTSTRAP and some JQUERY.
