The Story

Lyles European Dobermans is a small family business that is located in the suburbs of Chicago. They have been training Dobermans since 1978. In the mid 80’s They also showed, breed, raised American Pit Bull Terriers and American Staffordshire Terriers. They also started a dog club with the United Kennel Club on the island of Oahu. In 2006 They went back to their first love “The European Doberman”. This client was in need of a website to showcase and to help sell their doberman puppies. This site was created from scratch designed in Photoshop and developed with Dreamweaver using HTML, CSS, JQUERY, and a little bit of back-end programming using PHP for contact form validation.
